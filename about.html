The bursary scheme was introduced in 1990 as away to improve the economic situation in the country.

The scheme is aimed at bridging the gap between poverty and illiteracy. It was mainly designed to help

bright students who cannot afford to meet school fee requirements.

The scheme has seen an overly increase in student population in secondary and tertiary learning institutions.

This has also resulted in an increased literate workforce in industries and emergence of entrepreneurs.
